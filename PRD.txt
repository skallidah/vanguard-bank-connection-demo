DOCUMENT PURPOSE
----------------
This PRD describes the single end-to-end UI flow for the Demo Bank
Connection Demo app. It is written for LambdaTest KaneAI to generate one
positive test scenario covering the complete user journey from landing to
successful bank account submission.

  App URL:  https://vanguard-bank-connection-demo-production.up.railway.app
  GitHub:   https://github.com/skallidah/demo-bank-connection

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 1: APPLICATION OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The Demo Bank Connection simulates the bank account linking flow from
demo-app.com. A customer connects an external bank account to their Demo-App
investment accounts through a 4-step guided UI. At the end, the system confirms
the connection and initiates micro-deposit verification.

User journey:
  Step 1 — Landing page       (/):               Read intro, click CONTINUE
  Step 2 — Add Bank form      (/add-bank):        Enter bank and owner details
  Step 3 — Authorize Accounts (/authorize/<id>):  Confirm which investment accounts are linked
  Step 4 — Review             (/review/<id>):     Review summary and submit
  Step 5 — Success            (/success/<id>):    Confirmation with Bank Connection ID

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 3: END-TO-END TEST SCENARIO
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SCENARIO: Connect a new bank account — full happy path

  STEP 1 — Landing page
    1. Open https://vanguard-bank-connection-demo-production.up.railway.app/
    Expected:
      - Page heading "Connect your bank account" is visible
      - "Demo-App" logo is displayed
      - A 4-step ordered list describing the micro-deposit process is visible
      - A CONTINUE button is present
    2. Click CONTINUE

  STEP 2 — Add Bank form
    Expected after clicking CONTINUE:
      - Page heading "Add a new bank" is visible
    3. Enter 123456789 in the Routing number field
    4. Click or tab away from the Routing number field
    Expected:
      - Bank name field auto-populates with "Y Bank Test"
      - Correspondent routing number field shows "021000021"
      - Correspondent account number field shows "8675309001"
    5. Enter 11223344 in the Account number field
    6. Enter 11223344 in the Re-enter account number field
    7. Confirm Checking is selected for Account type (it is the default)
    8. Enter Elizabeth in the Owner first name field
    9. Enter Singleton in the Owner last name field
    10. Enter Primary Checking in the Bank nickname field
    11. Confirm the "Make this your primary bank" checkbox is checked (it is the default)
    12. Click CONTINUE

  STEP 3 — Authorize Accounts
    Expected after clicking CONTINUE:
      - Page heading "Authorize accounts" is visible
      - Bank information table shows:
          Bank name:              Y Bank Test
          Routing number:         123456789
          Account type:           CHECKING
          Account owners:         Elizabeth Singleton
          Correspondent routing:  021000021
          Correspondent account:  8675309001
      - "Accounts that will be authorized" section lists:
          Elizabeth Singleton — Brokerage Account — VA_80001 (Managed)
      - "Review additional accounts" section lists:
          Elizabeth Singleton — Traditional IRA — VA_80002 (Managed)
    13. Click REVIEW

  STEP 4 — Review
    Expected after clicking REVIEW:
      - Page heading "Review your information" is visible
      - Bank information table shows:
          Bank name:            Y Bank Test
          Routing number:       123456789
          Account type:         CHECKING
          Account owners:       Elizabeth Singleton
          Bank nickname:        Primary Checking
          Primary bank:         Yes
          Verification method:  MICRO_DEPOSIT
      - Authorized accounts section lists:
          Elizabeth Singleton — Brokerage Account — VA_80001 (Managed)
    14. Click SUBMIT

  STEP 5 — Success
    Expected after clicking SUBMIT:
      - Browser navigates to /success/<bank_connection_id>
      - A green checkmark (✓) is visible
      - Heading "Entry submitted successfully" is displayed
      - Text "Your bank account has been connected and micro-deposit
        verification has been initiated." is visible
      - Text "It may take 1–3 business days for the deposits to appear
        in your account." is visible
      - "Bank Connection ID: bc_" followed by alphanumeric characters is displayed
      - A START OVER button is present
