Connect your bank account

Quickly and easily move money between your bank and Demo-App.

Follow these steps to securely transfer money electronically.

  1. Enter your bank account information on the next page. You'll need your bank account and bank routing numbers—both can be found printed on your checks.
  2. We'll authenticate your bank information by making two small deposits into your bank account, which may take one to three business days to post to your account.
  3. Contact your bank or review your transaction history online to identify the two deposits from Demo-App.
  4. Complete the authentication process by logging on to vanguard.com, going to your bank information page, and entering the two amounts.

Important note: You can't move money from Demo-App to this bank until you've completed the micro-deposit process, which can take two to three business days, and we've validated your banking information, which generally takes 7 to 10 calendar days. All bank account owners can add this bank information to their Demo-App profiles, but should add it on different days to accurately identify the deposits associated with each profile.
